THE STORY

Road To Tech

My journey in tech began with a curiosity about how things work. I’ve always been drawn to the intersection of design and functionality, which naturally led me toward a career in IT.

Over the years, I’ve refined my ability to build intuitive, user-focused interfaces and solve technical problems that have a real, practical impact. I’ve learned that great front-end development isn’t just about writing clean code — it’s about empathy: understanding the person on the other side of the screen and building experiences that respect their time, attention, and expectations.

Today, I continue to grow across both design and development, always pushing to improve my craft and deliver solutions that are not only functional, but genuinely enjoyable to use. Each project represents an opportunity to learn, evolve, and create something meaningful.

The Turning Point

I transitioned into more complex projects, placing a stronger emphasis on responsive design and thoughtful user experience decisions.

Present Day

I currently focus on exploring front-end development through personal projects, refining my understanding of accessibility, performance, and visual design. Driven by curiosity and a love for learning, I continue to experiment, build, and improve—treating every project as an opportunity to grow.
